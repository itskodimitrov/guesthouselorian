If you want to get away from the hustle and bustle of the city, the village of Madjare is the perfect place. The crystal clear air, the silence, the clear waters of the river Iskar, everything leads you to a long-forgotten world of unity with nature. 10 km from the municipal center of Samokov, the village captivates by peace and comfort. There are ski slopes nearby for lovers of winter sports, and the resort of Borovets is a 20-minute drive away. And unlike expensive hotels, here you can afford a vacation at minimal prices.
